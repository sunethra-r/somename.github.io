Spinach soup
Prepared using spinach as primary ingredient. Good way to include healthy greens in your diet and is low in calories. Generally the combination of spinach goes well with cream but I have not used cream to this soup. I have added potatoes for the thickness.

Spinach Soup

Recipi
1. Heat up a pan and add 1 tsp of butter or oil, add 3 chopped garlic. Sauté till the garlic is lightly brown.
2. Add 1/2 cup of chopped onions. Sauté till the onions are softened.
3. Now add chopped potatoes and spinach to it. Sauté it well.
4. Add a cup of water and cook it for 5 minutes.
5. Wait until the cooked spinach is cooled and then blend it in mixer till smooth.
6. Add the blended mix in the same pan and cook it about 2 to 3 minutes.
7. Serve Spinach Soup with freshly crushed black pepper and salt.
You can add green chilies if you want spicy soup.

Carrot Soup:
The carrots provide beta carotene. These cooked together with aromatics like onions, garlic and fresh herbs. Properly cooked vegetable soup will last for 3 to 4 days in the refrigerator.

Carrot Soup

Recipi:
1. Take a pan add 1 tsp of butter or 1 tsp of extra-virgin olive oil.
2. Add chopped onions, garlic, parsley or cilantro cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
3. Now add the carrots and water, cook until very tender about 20 minutes.
4. Purée the soup in batches in a blender until smooth ( use caution when pureering hot liquids).
5. Add salt and fresh pepper to the soup.

Vegetable Cutlet:
Cutlet is derived from French côtelette, côte. A mash of vegetables coated with bread crumbs or rava (semolina). Simple, easy and delicious vegetable cutlet that can be served for a evening snack with a chutney or dip. A green chutney or a schezwan sauce goes best with these cutlet.

Vegetable Cutlet

Recipi:
1.Boil vegetables like potatoes , peas, carrots, cut french beans.
2.Mash them by hand or masher.
3.Add red chillies powder, salt , cut corainder, finely cut onions. Mash little until it is mixed well.
4.Make balls & pat them in dried, powdered bread crumbs or semolina.
5.Apply oil on both sides & heat on a hot plate ( Tawa in Hindi).
6.One may prepare chutney or eat it with tomato ketchup.
